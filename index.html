About Me

People like to label themselves primarily off the basis of their profession. It makes sense, but I think there are much better alternatives of describing oneself. For example, I would describe myself as a passionate learner. I like learning. My love of learning is the root of everything I do. My professions as a writer and programmer stem from my desire to learn. My love of books and passion for video game development are also a product of that same desire. The term “learner” not only defines what I do, but also who I am as a person. This is why I think it is better to describe myself as that instead of writing about how I am a programmer, writer, and web developer. Also I already used those terms at the top of the site and I didn’t want to be repetitive.
